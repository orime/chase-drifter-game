About Drift Car Multiplayer

Drift Car Multiplayer is an exciting drifting game that combines realistic physics with competitive multiplayer action. Get behind the wheel of high-performance vehicles and compete with players worldwide in this adrenaline-fueled racing experience.

With three unique racing cars to unlock and extensive customization options, you can create your perfect drifting machine. The game features stunning graphics, realistic car sounds, and fluid drifting mechanics that make every race exciting.

Choose between single-player mode for practice or jump into online multiplayer to compete with other players. With three different map options featuring varying conditions and atmospheres, every race offers a unique challenge.

How to Play

1

Controls

Use W-A-S-D keys to control your vehicle. Press C to change camera view and space to brake. Master these controls to perform perfect drifts.

2

Progression

Earn points and rewards by performing high-level drifts to unlock new cars and customization options for your vehicles.

3

Game Modes

Choose between single-player mode for practice or compete against other players in online multiplayer matches.
