About Chase Drifter

Chase Drifter is an exciting police chase racing game that puts you in the driver's seat of high-performance vehicles. Experience the thrill of drifting through city streets while evading pursuers in this adrenaline-fueled adventure.

With stunning graphics, realistic physics, and challenging gameplay, Chase Drifter offers an immersive racing experience that will test your reflexes and driving skills. Navigate through traffic, perform spectacular drifts, and outrun the police to become the ultimate street racer.

Whether you're a casual gamer or a racing enthusiast, Chase Drifter delivers an action-packed experience that will keep you coming back for more.

How to Play

1

Controls

Use arrow keys or WASD to control your vehicle. Press spacebar for handbrake to initiate drifts around corners.

2

Objectives

Complete tracks within the time limit, earn points for successful drifts, and avoid getting caught by police vehicles.

3

Scoring

Earn points based on drift quality, duration, and speed. Chain multiple drifts together for combo multipliers.
